Guidance notes
From 2018, Wellcome has allowed organisations to request to retain some, or all, of Wellcome's revenue share (if not already paid to Wellcome) to apply it to new translational activities or to support access to healthcare interventions. A condition of any such revenue retention is that you report on your use of the retained funds. As ${e://Field/organisation} was allowed to retain revenue in ${e://Field/Previous%20year}, please set out full details in this report of:
  1. Section 1: The amounts retained;
  2. Section 1: The amounts spent to date;
  3. Section 1: Any unspent amounts (to be returned to Wellcome);
  4. Section 2: What the retained funds have been spent on;
  5. Section 2: A confirmation that this spend matches the uses referred to when the request to retain the funds was originally made; and
  6. Section 3: How the funded activities have supported translation or access to healthcare interventions, with reference to any success metrics given in your original application

Unless you have agreed a different reporting period with Wellcome, you must submit your report by ${e://Field/Deadline}1 .

Personal information provided by you shall only be collected, stored and used by Wellcome to the extent that the law allows.  Most commonly we will use it to analyse what Wellcome funds and how we fund it.  See our Wellcome Grants Privacy Statement for full details.

Wellcome will treat the contents of your report as confidential.
 

1Failure to submit reports on time may impact on our decision, if you apply to retain Wellcome's share of revenue in the future.
