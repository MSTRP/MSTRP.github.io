Rationale
The Grant Conditions require TPA grant holders to submit an annual written progress report to Wellcome Trust evidencing progress made against the agreed plans, budget spend and impact measurement. The Annual Report is also an opportunity for you to share details of outcomes and achievements resulting from TPA support.

Conditions
Annual Report must be submitted by the date specified in the request email. Failure to submit a report may cause delay in the next payment.
